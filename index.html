Who we are

Kibo AI Solutions is a Brazilian AI company specializing in the development of intelligent products and the application of AI to existing businesses. We bridge the gap between cutting-edge AI research and real-world business impact.

Our Vision

We believe AI should be precise, accessible, and built with intention. We are not a generic agency — we are specialists who understand both the technical depth and the human context where AI operates.

Innovation

We push the boundaries of what AI can do, staying at the forefront of model capabilities and engineering best practices.

Precision

Every model, every pipeline, every interaction is designed to be accurate and reliable. We obsess over quality.

Accessibility

AI should serve everyone. We build systems that are understandable, fair, and usable by real people in real workflows.

Partnership

We work alongside our clients as collaborators, not vendors. Your success is our technical north star.

Our flagship project is RadioeXperience, a radiology teaching platform that combines structured medical content with AI-powered search and diagnosis assistance through our proprietary assistant ARIA.

We partner with healthcare institutions, innovative startups, and established businesses that want to leverage artificial intelligence as a competitive edge — safely, practically, and at scale.
